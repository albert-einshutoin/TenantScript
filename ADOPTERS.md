## Share an adoption report

Use the [adopter report pull-request template](.github/PULL_REQUEST_TEMPLATE/adopter-report.md) to
add a public entry. State what may be published, remove customer or tenant details, and choose broad
usage bands when exact counts are sensitive. Maintainers verify consent and sanitization, not the
commercial identity of a submitter.

If you cannot publish a name, use the
[private-safe feedback template](https://github.com/albert-einshutoin/TenantScript/issues/new?template=feedback.yml)
and choose the anonymous option. Do not include an organization, customer, deployment URL, account
identifier, credential, or tenant data. Feedback issues are public; suspected vulnerabilities must
use the private process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Telemetry is a separate, default-off mechanism and never enrolls an adopter. Its exact anonymous
schema and limitations are documented in [Opt-in telemetry and privacy](docs/privacy/telemetry.md).
